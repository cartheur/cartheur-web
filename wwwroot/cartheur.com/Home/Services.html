iCybie support services

Cartheur provides a cartridge writing service for CROMINST or other files. We charge 20€ for a session, that is, if you have multiple cartridges, we will burn up to five for the same fee. We can't be responsible for the quality of cartridges, but will do our best to get you up and running. From experience, the SST chips are quite robust, not unlike its cybernetic cousin. If you want an express service, please let us know at the time of your order.

The binary images we have in stock are:

  • Borg
  • Cartman
  • Generic (to 2.02)
  • Goddard
  • K9
  • Klingon
  • Mojo
  • Pooh
  • Scooby
  • Tigger
  • zCybie
Else, if you have custom images, we can burn these also.

When you pay for the service, please put in your correct delivery address. We will then invoice you for the cost of signed-for return shipping (and a small fee for handling) to your country from the European Union. We would then ask for you to send us your cartridges for burning. Please send an email and let us know what to burn for you including custom YICT images if you provide them. We have the proper CROMNIST bin files if you are doing a SiC upgrade.

Any questions, please feel free to contact us.
